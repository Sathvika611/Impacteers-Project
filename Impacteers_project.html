TRIBUTE
“THE GREATEST TRIBUTE IS SHOWING GRATITUDE”

India is a very beautiful country. It is a mystical place. No wonder they call it “Incredible”.I am proud to say that I belong to the land of many great people. India has produced several famous personalities that have excelled in their field. These Indian heroes have battled against all odds and have reached the pinnacle of success by their courage, determination and perseverance. Their lives have inspired a whole generation of Indians and continue to inspire millions of Indians and others all around the world. These include people from all walks of life such as freedom fighters, leaders, scientists, writers, painters, musicians, social reformers and entrepreneurs. Many of their sacrifices went unnoticed but some of them found their way into history books and are even remembered today with great pride and honour. As a true Indian its our duty to take the honour of paying tribute to the famous personalities of the country. Here's a look at some of those great and famous personalities because of whom we stand on the brink of social, political and economical greatness.
